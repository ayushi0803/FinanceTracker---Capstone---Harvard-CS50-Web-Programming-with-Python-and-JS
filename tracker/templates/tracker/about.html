About Ayushi

Hey there! I'm Ayushi, and I built this web application to help you manage your expenses and keep track of your money. It's designed to be easy-to-use, allowing you to:

  • Add money to your account
  • Track your expenses
  • View your total balance and expenses

I built this as a project for my CS50W course from Harvard, and I hope it helps you stay financially organized!
